Milkshakes

Milkshakes are indulgent, creamy treats that combine ice cream, milk, and flavorings for a rich, sweet experience. They typically have a thick, velvety texture, often topped with whipped cream, chocolate sauce, or sprinkles.

Ingredients:

  • Ice cream
  • Milk
  • Chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream
  • Toppings

Steps:

  1. Add the ice cream to the blender.
  2. Add the milk to the blender.
  3. Blend on low until smooth.
  4. Pour into a cup and garnish with whipped cream.
  5. Add some sprinkles.
  6. And add a cherry if desired.
