Cookies

Cookies are delightful baked treats that come in a variety of shapes, sizes, and flavors. They can range from soft and chewy to crispy and crunchy, often featuring a rich sweetness and a buttery flavor. Classic varieties include chocolate chip, oatmeal raisin, and sugar cookies, each with its unique texture and taste

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • Â¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Sift flour and brown sugar into a bowl; stir in eggs.
  3. Mix butter into flour mixture until dough is creamy; fold in chocolate chips. Spoon
  4. Spoon dough onto a baking sheet, keeping them about 3 inches apart.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until slightly brown, 10 to 15 minutes then cool.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
