Crispy Nachos

Nachos are a beloved Mexican-inspired snack or appetizer, consisting of crispy tortilla chips topped with a variety of savory ingredients. At the heart of the dish are the tortilla chips, which are typically made from corn and either fried or baked to a golden crisp. These chips form the base, ready to be loaded with flavorful toppings

Ingredients:

  • 500g lean beef or lamb mince
  • 420g can Wattie's Mild Chilli Beans
  • 400g can Wattie's Mexican-style Tomatoes
  • Corn chips
  • 1 cup grated Edam cheese
  • Â½ cup lite sour cream (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp chopped spring onions (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a non-stick pan heat a dash of oil and brown mince in batches.
  2. Return mince to the pan, add Wattie's Mild Chilli Beans and Wattie's Mexican-style Tomatoes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Place corn chips in individual bowls, spoon in the mince and sprinkle with cheese. Top with sour cream and chopped spring onions (if desired).
  4. Serve with green salad and enjoy!
