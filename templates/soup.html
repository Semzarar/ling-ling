Autumn Soup

Autumn soup is a cozy, hearty dish made with seasonal ingredients that evoke the essence of fall. It typically includes a variety of root vegetables like butternut squash, sweet potatoes, carrots, and pumpkins, which give the soup a rich, velvety texture and natural sweetness.

Ingredients:

  • A splash of olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion
  • Ground turkey but you can also use ground beef or chicken
  • Garlic powder
  • Oregano
  • Carrots
  • Half of a garlic head
  • Tomato paste
  • Fire roasted diced tomatoes
  • Low sodium chicken stock
  • Fresh cheese tortellini
  • Coconut milk
  • Spinach
  • One lemon
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Saut√© ground turkey, onion, and seasonings, then add carrots and garlic.
  2. Add tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and chicken stock. Bring to a boil, then add tortellini and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Turn off heat and stir in coconut milk, spinach, lemon juice, and extra salt.
