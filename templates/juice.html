Freshly Squeezed Juice

Juice is a liquid extracted from fruits, vegetables, or other plants, known for its refreshing taste and vibrant colors. It can range from sweet to tangy, depending on the ingredients. Freshly squeezed juice often has a bright, lively flavor and a natural sweetness, while store-bought varieties might have added sugars or preservatives.

Ingredients:

  • 1 small fennel bulb
  • 1 apple, cored and chopped
  • 85g frozen blueberries
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Trim the top and bottom from the fennel bulb and cut into pieces.
  2. Put the fennel, apple and frozen blueberries through your juicer following the manufacturers instructions, then stir in the lemon juice and serve immediately.
  3. Serve between glasses and enjoy!.
