Beef Burrito

A beef burrito is a flavorful, hearty Mexican dish consisting of a large flour tortilla wrapped around a filling of seasoned beef, rice, beans, and other tasty ingredients. The beef is often ground or shredded and cooked with a blend of spices such as cumin, chili powder, garlic, and paprika, giving it a rich, savory flavor with a slight kick.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp of sunflower oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, very finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp of ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp of ground coriander
  • Small pinch of cayenne pepper ( add more if you want it spicier)
  • 1 tsp of dried oregano
  • A pinch of golden caster sugar
  • 200ml beef stock
  • 1 tbsp of wine vinegar or cider vinegar
  • 400g can of chopped tomatoes
  • 400g can of black beans or kidney beans, with the can water
  • 8 flour or corn tortillas
  • 500g cooked rice, or our Mexican tomato rice

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan but a casserole is ideal. Fry the onions for 8 mins, then add the garlic, spices and oregano and cook for 1 min.
  2. Crumble over the mince and sizzle for 5 mins, stirring, until browned.
  3. Stir in the sugar and leave for a minute, then splash in the vinegar and pour in the tomatoes.
  4. Simmer for 5 mins then tip in the beans and the water from the can. Season, stir and simmer everything for 20 mins until the beef is in a thick gravy.
  5. The sauce can be prepared up to 2 days ahead, chilled and reheated with a splash of water or frozen for 6 months.
  6. To make the burritos, heat the tortillas following pack instructions.
  7. Pile some rice and beef sauce along each tortilla and scatter over your choice of topping.
  8. Fold over the ends and roll up to seal. Secure by wrapping with foil if you want. Eat immediately.
  9. Serve the burritos in plates and enjoy!
