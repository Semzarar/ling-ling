Doughnuts

Doughnuts are sweet, deep-fried pastries that are soft and airy on the inside, with a golden, slightly crispy exterior. They come in two primary shapes: the classic ring doughnut with a hole in the center and the filled doughnut, which is stuffed with various fillings like jam, custard, or cream.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp) active dry yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 large egg
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Mix warm milk, water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, butter, egg, and salt, then mix into a dough. Knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth. Let rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll out dough, cut into circles, and let them rise for 30 minutes.
  4. Heat oil and fry the doughnuts for 1-2 minutes per side, until golden brown. Drain.
  5. Pipe in chocolate filling and dust with powdered sugar if desired.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
