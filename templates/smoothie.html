Fruit Smoothie

A fruit smoothie is a refreshing blend of vibrant colors and flavors, typically made by combining various fruits with a liquid base like yogurt, milk, or juice. Imagine a creamy, luscious concoction filled with ripe bananas, juicy strawberries, and tangy blueberries, all swirling together.

Ingredients:

  • Frozen Fruit
  • Greek yogurt
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Put the water in the blender.
  2. Add the frozen fruit and yogurt..
  3. Put the blender lid back on (very important) and blend for a few minutes until it is smooth.
  4. Serve with a straw for extra fun. Enjoy!
