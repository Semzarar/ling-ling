Vanilla Cupcakes

Vanilla cupcakes are delightful, fluffy treats with a light, tender crumb. They typically feature a rich, buttery flavor complemented by the sweetness of vanilla, creating a comforting and classic taste. The batter is often made with simple ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and vanilla extract, resulting in a creamy and smooth texture.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cups (325g) all purpose flour
  • 2 cups (414g) sugar
  • 3 tsp baking powder (NOT baking soda)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) vegetable oil
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs/li>
  • 1 cup (240ml) water
  • Vanilla frosting
  • 1 cup (224g) unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 4 cups (460g) powdered sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 to 3 tbsp (30-45ml) water or milk
  • A pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C & prepare 12 paper cupcake cases in patty pans.
  2. Cream Butter and White Sugar.
  3. Add eggs and mix well. Add vanilla essence.
  4. Add Fresh Milk alternatively with Grade Flour and Baking Powder.
  5. Spoon evenly into paper cases and bake at 200°C for 15 - 20 minutes.
  6. Ice/decorate as desired.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
