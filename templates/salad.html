Prawn Cocktail Salad

Prawn Cocktail Salad is a fresh, vibrant dish that combines succulent prawns with crisp vegetables and a creamy, tangy dressing. It’s a modern take on the classic prawn cocktail, served in a salad form, making it both refreshing and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 500g of jumbo prawns, (Cooked and Peeled), thawed
  • 2 Tbsp of garlic butter
  • 3 avocados
  • 1 head of cos lettuce
  • Marie Rose dressing
  • ½ cup of Mayonnaise
  • 3 Tbsp of tomato sauce
  • 1 Tbsp of lime juice
  • 2 tsp of Worcestershire sauce
  • ½ tsp of paprika

Steps:

  1. Wash and slice cos lettuce then arrange on a platter. Thinly slice avocado and lay on top of the lettuce.
  2. Mix all of the ingredients together for the Marie Rose sauce and chill until ready to use.
  3. Fry prawns for 1 minute on each side in sizzling garlic butter. Arrange on top of the lettuce and avocado.
  4. Serve with dressing drizzled over the salad and lime wedges and enjoy!
